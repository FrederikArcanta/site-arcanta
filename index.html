One platform, full visibility

Arcanta is a multi-tenant analytics platform that unifies your catering POS data with real-time presence and occupancy metrics. Upload your data, and let Arcanta surface the trends, patterns, and opportunities hiding in your operations.

Whether you manage a single company restaurant or oversee catering across multiple sites, Arcanta scales with you — offering role-based access, multi-language support, and dedicated dashboards for every level of your organization.

Capabilities

Everything you need to manage your catering

From high-level KPIs to granular product mix analysis, Arcanta gives facility managers the full picture.

Guest Flow & Trends

Track guest counts, passage volumes, and peak hours throughout the day. Spot lunch rush patterns, weekly rhythms, and seasonal shifts to plan ahead with confidence.

Menu & Product Intelligence

Dive deep into menu item performance, category breakdowns, and combo analysis. Understand which dishes drive satisfaction and optimize your offering to reduce waste.

Occupancy & Utilisation

Measure seat utilisation and occupancy rates in real time. Compare performance across locations and time periods to optimize seating capacity and staffing levels.

Interactive Dashboards

Clean, intuitive dashboards designed for facility managers. Heatmaps, comparison views, and drilldowns — get to the insight without needing a data team.

Multi-site Management

Manage one restaurant or an entire portfolio of catering locations. Role-based access ensures the right people see the right data — from site level to boardroom.

Easy Data Upload

Import catering POS transactions and headcount data via CSV or Excel. Smart column mapping and validation ensure clean data flows into your dashboards automatically.
