Arcanta

Your analytical dashboard for catering performance

Arcanta is a self-service analytics platform where facility managers periodically upload POS and headcount data to track KPIs, uncover trends, and perform deep analysis. No data team required — just upload, map your columns, and go.

Self-service Data Upload

Import POS transactions and headcount data via CSV or Excel. Smart column mapping lets you match your data to the right fields — no IT support needed.

KPI Tracking & Trends

Monitor guest counts, revenue, average spend, and seat utilisation over time. Spot weekly rhythms, seasonal patterns, and anomalies at a glance.

Menu & Product Analysis

Understand which dishes perform, which combos guests prefer, and how the product mix evolves. Use hard data to optimize the menu together with your caterer.

Deep-dive Dashboards

Heatmaps, intraday charts, comparison views, and drilldowns. Designed for facility managers who want to go beyond surface-level reports.

Caterer Accountability

Stop relying on your caterer's reports alone. Arcanta gives you independent, verifiable data to benchmark performance and hold informed SLA discussions.

Multi-site Management

Manage one company restaurant or dozens of locations. Role-based access ensures the right people see the right data — from site level to boardroom.

Caterpulse

Real-time occupancy tracking for your catering locations

Caterpulse is a lightweight occupancy measurement system that shows you — and your employees — how busy your restaurant is right now. With its own dashboard, embeddable widget, and per-location insights, it helps guests and managers make smarter decisions in the moment.

Live Occupancy Dashboard

A dedicated dashboard showing real-time occupancy per location. See how many guests are present, how full the space is, and how the current hour compares to normal.

Embeddable Widget

A compact widget you can embed on your company intranet, internal website, or digital signage. Employees see at a glance whether the restaurant is quiet, busy, or full.

Broadcast to Employees

Use Caterpulse to inform employees about current occupancy levels. Help spread lunch traffic, reduce queues, and improve the guest experience in your building.

Per-location Insights

Each catering location gets its own occupancy view. Compare live status across sites and understand which locations are under pressure at any given moment.

Lightweight & Easy to Deploy

Caterpulse is designed to be simple. Connect a presence sensor, configure your location, and you're live. No heavy infrastructure, no complex setup.

Better Guest Experience

When employees know which restaurant is quieter, they naturally spread out. Less congestion, shorter queues, and a more pleasant lunch break for everyone.
